Binary elliptic orbit simulation

Physical model

Two stars move in the center-of-mass frame under Newtonian gravity. The initial separation is treated as the periapsis distance, and the initial speed is chosen from the vis-viva relation for an elliptic orbit.

$$r_p = a(1-e),\qquad v_{\mathrm{rel}} = \sqrt{GM\left(\frac{2}{r_p}-\frac{1}{a}\right)}$$

Numerical method

The trajectory is evolved with leapfrog / velocity Verlet integration. The panel on the right reports total energy, energy range, and maximum relative drift so you can monitor numerical stability while adjusting parameters.
