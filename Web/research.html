Publication under Prepartion

  • Pattern Formation and Dynamics in Quantum System

    • My research focuses on bridging the gap between quantum dynamics and non-linear phenomena to reveal pattern formation mechanisms in quantum systems. Employing the quantum trajectories method and phase representation, I investigate pattern formation in both two-site and many-site quantum systems. Anticipated outcomes involve characterizing patterns in phase space, comprehending non-linear effects, and assessing the influence of wave function patterns on quantum statistics. In essence, this study can contribute to a more profound understanding of the interplay between non-linear dynamics, pattern formation, and statistical mechanics in quantum systems.
